Our Story

We are We Strive

We Strive is focused on meeting the needs of people around the world, and enabling them to achieve their dreams. Created in 2017, this organization is the result of several years of hard work, unprecedented community input, and dozens of meetings.

We Strive sets out long-term vision, policies, and strategic actions for our patrons with a time horizon for 2050.

Our plan of action evolves based on the needs and transforming environment around the globe.

The road map to our future and annual budget sets forth the funding needed to make our plans a reality.
