About Us

At We Strive, we focus on meeting the needs of people worldwide and empowering them to achieve their dreams. Since our founding in 2017, we’ve built our organization on years of hard work, deep community engagement, and countless strategic meetings.

We’re committed to setting a long-term vision, creating thoughtful policies, and taking strategic actions to support our patrons. Our plan looks ahead to 2050, allowing us to adapt to the ever-changing world and stay responsive to new challenges as they arise.

To make our vision a reality, we set a clear annual roadmap and budget to secure the funding needed for our initiatives. We believe in the power of community, and we invite you to join us in this journey. You can support our cause by volunteering at our events or making a We Striveion.

Your contributions will help enrich the lives of students and young minds, empowering them to reach their full potential.

Together, we can create a brighter future..
