Kickstart Guide

HOW CAN YOU START?

First of all, welcome to our youth organization! To get started, you should first read up on our goals and mission. Understanding what drives us will help you connect with our vision and the impact we strive to make in our community. We’re excited to have you on this journey with us!

HOW YOU CAN CONTRIBUTE

Here are easy ways to join, contribute, and better yourself for personal goals, whether for college or self-improvement. Set clear goals, seek relevant resources, and engage with like-minded. Volunteering and taking workshops can also foster growth. Every small step leads to progress—read on for more!

Volunteering with Strive for Hours is a rewarding experience that not only allows you to make a positive impact in the community but also lets you connect with like-minded individuals while having a great time.

Take a look at our vast variety of clubs in the services catalog and explore your interests! Join a few clubs and grow together!

Follow us on Facebook and Instagram to get updated on our community events. Come participate and help out our community at every chance you get through our events!

Come join our events, volunteer, make life-long friends, and enjoy your time with We Strive! At We Strive consider us one big family!
